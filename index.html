Simple Rules of Thumb Calculator

Simple calculator for leveling ancients in the game Clicker Heroes.

Idle Builds - Enter your Siyalatas Level

$$Morgulis = (Siya+1)^2$$
$$Gold\ ancients = .927 * Siya$$
$$Solomon = Minimum( Siya, 1.15 * \ln{(3.25 * Siya^2)}^{0.4} * Siya^{0.8} )$$
$$Iris = 5*\lfloor\frac{371 * \ln({Siya}) - 2075}{5}\rfloor - 2$$
